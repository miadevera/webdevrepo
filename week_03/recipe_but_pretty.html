Mia's Pumpkin Pancakes

This recipe is good for all times of the year but it is just timely that it is finally fall!
You can either use canned pumpkin or freshly purreed pumpkin, up to you!
Can I freeze this recipe? Yes you can... and up to 2 years

Ingredients!

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • <3 tablepoons brown sugar/li>
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • <1 teaspoon baking soda/li>
  • 1 teasoon allspice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • cooking spray

Instructions!

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Stir milk, pumpkin, egg. oil, and vinegar together in a large bowl until well combined
  3. 3. Whisk flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, allspice, cinnamon, ginger, and salt together in a separate bowl. Add to pumpkin mixture; whisk just until combined 4. Spray a griddle or frying pan with cooking spray; heat over medium-high heat 5. Pour 3 to 4 tablespoons batter for each pancake onto the hot griddle and flatten batter slightly with a spoon 6. Cook until small bubbles appear, about 2 minutes 7. Flip and cook until golden brown, about 2 more minutes. Repeat with remaining batter 8. Serve hot and enjoy!
